Slides

Types of Slides

There are two types of slides: straight and trigger. Straight slides are used to change the pitch of the instrument, while trigger slides are used to change the pitch of the instrument by a specific interval.

Bells

Bells are essential to create the signature trombone sound.

Mouthpieces

Mouthpieces are a replaceable part of the trombone that you buzz into.
